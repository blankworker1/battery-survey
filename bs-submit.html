The recording is the evidence Every submitted record must have a screen recording showing the SOH screen, the cell voltages screen, and the harvester reading the values aloud. Without a valid recording the submission will be rejected. For harvests on other people's vehicles, complete the pre-harvest consent form before plugging in.
