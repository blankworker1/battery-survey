Before you go

Check your kit

Tick everything before you leave. A missing item means a wasted visit.

Android phone charged — CanZE installed and opened at least once
ELM327 v1.5 Bluetooth dongle in your bag — not Wi-Fi, not v2.1
Screen recording enabled and tested on your phone
Address and owner contact number confirmed
Vehicle confirmed as Kangoo ZE (not petrol Kangoo)
Battery Survey submission form bookmarked on phone
Important ELM327 v2.1 has a firmware bug that breaks CanZE's BMS connection on the Kangoo ZE. Must be v1.5 Bluetooth only.
Download the app CanZE app can be downloaded from the Google Play Store. For more details check out canze.fisch.lu.
2
At the vehicle

Find the OBD port

🔌
Location — Kangoo ZE

Under the driver's side dashboard, left of the steering column, near the fuse cover. 16-pin trapezoid socket. Plug in firmly — dongle LED will light within a few seconds.

1
Ignition to ready mode

Key to position 2 or press start without brake. Dashboard lights on. Do not drive the vehicle.

2
Plug in the dongle

Seat it firmly. LED on dongle lights up. If no light after 10 seconds, reseat it.

3
Pair via Bluetooth

Phone Settings → Bluetooth → pair to OBDII or V-LINK. PIN: 1234 or 0000. Do this before opening CanZE.

3
Connect

Run the app

1
Open CanZE

Settings → confirm adapter is Bluetooth ELM, vehicle is Kangoo ZE.

2
Connect to dongle

Tap Connect. Takes 10–20 seconds. Status bar turns green when connected.

3
Open Battery screens

Main menu → Battery. You need: Battery screen, Cell Detail screen, Charge History screen.

If CanZE won't connect Wait 30 seconds, retry. Still failing: unplug dongle, wait 10 seconds, reseat, retry. Three failed attempts = still a valid submission — record a video showing the attempt and note it in the form.
4
Evidence

Record it

Start recording before you open CanZE. Read every value aloud. Do not stop recording until all three screens are captured.

1
Start screen recording

Swipe down notification bar → Screen Record → confirm audio is on.

2
Battery screen — read aloud

SOH %, battery capacity (AHr or kWh), Hx value, pack voltage. Pause on each figure.

3
Cell Detail screen — read aloud

Highest and lowest cell voltages. Cell delta. Call out any red or yellow highlighted cells explicitly.

4
Charge History screen — read aloud

Total charge count, rapid charge count, any temperature warnings shown.

5
Stop and check the file

Play back the video. Confirm app is visible and voice is audible. Do not leave until you have checked this.

Submit the raw file Do not edit or trim the recording. If you made a mistake, note it in the form — do not cut it out. Edited recordings will be rejected.
5
Reference

What to look for

You don't need to interpret the results — just record them accurately. Use this as a reference.

SOH %
State of Health — remaining capacity relative to new.
75%+ Good 60–75% Fair <60% Poor
Hx
Internal resistance index — lower means higher resistance and faster degradation.
80%+ Good 60–80% Fair <60% Poor
Cell delta
Voltage gap between highest and lowest cell — large delta = cell imbalance.
<20mV Good 20–50mV Fair >50mV Poor
Rapid charge count
DC rapid charges taken — high counts accelerate degradation on older ZE packs.
<50 Good 50–100 Fair >100 High
Pack voltage
Total pack voltage at current charge level — record and report exactly.
Record all values
Red cells or unusual readings Do not intervene. Note exactly what you saw in the submission form. Your role is observation — not diagnosis or repair.
6
Submit

Send it back

Submit before you leave the area. Do not wait until you get home.

1
Open the submission form

Battery Survey submission form — have your harvester ID ready.

2
Enter all readings

SOH, Hx, AHr, pack voltage, cell delta, charge counts, temperature readings. Enter exactly what the app showed — do not round figures.

3
Upload the screen recording

Attach the raw video file. Max 500MB. If larger, contact the Battery Survey team before submitting.

4
Note anything unusual

Use the notes field — slow connection, warning lights, anything the owner mentioned about the vehicle's history.

5
Submit — save your reference number

You'll get a reference number immediately. Save it. Your reward is processed against this reference within 24 hours.

Questions? If anything is unclear or something unexpected happens, message the Battery Survey team using the contact in your onboarding email. Do not guess. A bad battery result is just as valuable as a good one — honest data is the whole point.
