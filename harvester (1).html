The role

What a harvester actually does

A harvester collects standardised battery health data from a supported electric vehicle using a free diagnostic app and a small Bluetooth dongle. The data goes into the Battery Survey database — a permanent, public, openly licensed record that anyone can look up.

During the MVP stage, all submissions come from owners testing their own Nissan Leaf. You plug in the dongle, screen-record the key Battery Management System screens in LeafSpy Pro, and submit the data through the Battery Survey form. No travel, no strangers' cars, no complications.

Process

How a self-submit works

Five steps from dongle to reward. Steps 2 to 5 take around 20 minutes at your vehicle.

1
Once only
Register and pair your dongle

Submit your harvester registration below. You'll receive onboarding instructions and a dongle pairing guide by email.

2
Each harvest
Plug in the dongle and start recording

With the vehicle in ready mode and stationary, plug the KW902 into the OBD port (under the steering column). Open LeafSpy Pro and wait for the connection. Start your screen recording.

3
Each harvest
Read the battery screens

Navigate to the Battery screen, the Cell Detail screen, and the History screen. Read each key value aloud on camera — SOH percentage, cell delta, rapid charge count. The recording is your evidence.

4
Each harvest
Submit the data

Fill in the Battery Survey submission form at bs-submit. Enter the values from the app, upload the screen recording, and confirm submission. You'll receive a reference number by return.

5
Each harvest
Record published — reward paid

The Battery Survey team reviews the submission within 24 hours. If the data passes quality checks, the record is published and your £15 reward is paid by bank transfer. The record is permanent and publicly accessible at batterysurvey.info.

Higher-reputation harvesters get priority allocation of paid harvest requests as volume grows. There is no ceiling — if you're good at this and available, you can do as many harvests as come your way.
